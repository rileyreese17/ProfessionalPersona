Biography

A short portrait of where I am now and where I'm headed next.

I'm a Business Economics student at the University of Arizona's Eller College of Management, preparing for law school. My view of good advocacy is simple: it starts with careful analysis and clear communication, and it holds up because of integrity.

My background combines quantitative business training with hands-on work. As a logistics intern, I led a nationwide operational analysis and brought cost-saving recommendations to senior management. Earlier jobs in service taught me to listen before acting and to solve problems under pressure, with the kind of follow-through that earns trust. Those habits of rigor and reliability carry into everything I take on.

I built this portfolio for a law school admissions reader, so it does more than list accomplishments. It shows how I see myself, how others see me, and how I'm bringing those two views into line as I head toward law.

  • Clear, persuasive communicator
  • Service-oriented leader
